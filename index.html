Features

Everything a busy leader needs.

From the booking page to the boardroom, LeaderCal handles the scheduling so your team can focus on the work that matters.

Leadership booking profiles

Executive-facing pages with bio, availability, and an intent selector for 1:1, coaching, or panel.

Team scheduling

Round-robin and collective scheduling with department-level routing rules for any org chart.

Custom event types

Executive 1:1 (30 min), Strategy Session (60 min), Board Prep (90 min) — pre-built and editable.

Embeddable widget

Drop a white-label <LeaderCal /> component into any intranet, leadership page, or microsite.

Two-way calendar sync

Google Calendar and Outlook in real time, with conflict detection and proper time-zone handling.

Auto video links

Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams links generated automatically for every booking.

CRM hooks

Push new bookings into Salesforce, HubSpot, or Notion as tasks, deals, or action items.

Slack & email notifications

Real-time Slack DMs to leaders, and branded confirmation emails powered by Postmark.

Availability intelligence

AI suggests the right meeting windows based on energy patterns, time-zone spread, and meeting load.

Booking analytics

Track meeting volume, response rate, no-show rate, and your top meeting requesters per leader.

VIP priority queue

Dedicated booking lanes for board members and key executives that bypass standard availability.

Buffer & focus time

Auto-block recovery after back-to-back meetings and enforce no-meeting mornings as policy.
